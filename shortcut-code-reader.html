The Shortcut Application

Image Recognition and
QR Code Scanning

Shortcut uses ultra-fast image-recognition technology. It connects real-world such as magazines, newspapers, billboards, products and other Shortcut-enabled items to online experiences. It also reads QR codes.

You can easily share what you see with friends via Facebook, Twitter, Email or SMS. And your Shortcuts are stored to the app, so you can alwasy go back to them.
