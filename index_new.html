How it works

  1. Create a Shortcut to link users to content online and in mobile apps. We generate a single link that you can inlude in your marketing campaigns. When a user clicks on your Shortcut, it opens your content online or in the specified app. When the app is not installed, it links the user to the store.
  2. Use Shortcut for mobile marketing campaigns, social campaigns, newsletters, user invites, QR Code campaigns, image recognition, or beacon campaigns.
  3. Measure and analyze the impact of your campaigns in a single online tool. This provides you with the best overview on all your marketing activities.

Super Powers


Short URLs

Every Shortcut is a unique short URL that is created for you to use in your campaigns. For every maketing campaign you only need one short link to which you can add superpowers. Even if the landing page for your campaign changes the short link doesn't, you can manage everything in the Shortcut Manager.

Mobile Deep Linking

Mobile deep links are smarter links. They detect if the user has your app installed or not and directs your customer to the right content. We recognize the device and link your users to a specific location within your mobile app. Drive app downloads and user experience with mobile deep links.

Post Install Deep Linking

We do deferred deep linking so that downloading your app doesn’t interrupt your customers user journey. We make sure your customer lands on the content they wanted to see when clicking on your Shortcut link, even if they download your app in between. Get a better user experience with deferred deep links.

Print to Mobile

With QR-codes and image recognition you can get on the mobile of your offline user. Wit each Shortcut you create you will get a unique QR-Code that you can use in your print advertising. If the landing page behind your QR-Code changes you can manage this easily in our Shortcut Manager without making any changes to the QR-Code itself.

Implement our SDK in order use use QR-Codes and image recognition in your own app.

In-store Push Notifications

With Beacons you can send push notifications to your in-store customers. Send push notifications from your app about for example current promotions when your customer is in your shop or close by. Increase sales and customer experience in your brick-and-mortar shop and get more re-engaged app users.

Analytics

In The Shortcut Manager you gan measure the success of your campaigns and your links. Analyze your success with information about the amount of visitors, the traffic sources and the user locations.
