The Shortcut Reader App

Image Recognition, QR Code Scanning, and iBeacons, all in one app

You can use the Shortcut Reader App for your campaigns or make your own reader with our SDK.

Shortcut uses ultra-fast image-recognition technology. It connects offline media such as magazines, newspapers, billboards, products and other Shortcut-enabled items to online experiences. It also reads QR codes and iBeacons.

You can easily share your scans with friends via Facebook, Twitter, Email or SMS. All Shortcuts are stored to the app, so you can alwasy go back to them.
